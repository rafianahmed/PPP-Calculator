Glossary (what the symbols mean)
  • S0 = spot exchange rate today
  • S1 = expected spot later (PPP/IFE expectation)
  • F = forward rate
  • π = inflation over the period
  • i = nominal interest rate over the period
  • q = real exchange rate / competitiveness measure
  • ρ = real interest rate
